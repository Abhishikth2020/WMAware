Addressing Waste Management and Plastic Pollution

According to Union Minister of State for Environment,
Forests and Climate Change, Prakash Javedkar, 62 million
tonnes of waste is generated annually in the country at
present out of which 5.6 million tonnes is plastic waste,
0.17 million tonnes is biomedical waste, hazardous waste
generation is 7.90 million tonnes per annum and 15 lakh
tonnes is e-waste.
