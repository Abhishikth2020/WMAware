Examples of action taken against Plastic Pollution

Example 1: European Parliament
The EP voted to ban single use plastics (straws, stirrers and other products). The ban would take force in 2021 in all European Union states with the UK also being involved. Polystyrene cups and oxo-degradable plastics would also be banned. The Parliament aims for 25% of plastic to be recyclable in 2025 and 90% of plastic to be recyclable in 2029.


Example 2: Disney
TBy mid-2019, The Walt Disney Company intended to ban 175 million straws and 13 million stirrers in theme parks annually. Also, Disney intends to eliminate polystyrene cups and implement reusable shopping bags in their theme parks/resorts. Instead, bamboo and paper straws would be provided.


Example 3: Chile
A bill was signed which banned the use of plastic bags across the country due to plastic pollution on land and in water. The bill was ratified (given formal consent and signed) on July 6th, 2018. This bill pressured small businesses and retailers to phase out plastic bags giving 2 years and 6 months respectively.
