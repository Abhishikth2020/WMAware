Conclusion and Final Thoughts

Waste management in my community













Based on the research provided and elaborated, the use of waste management strategies and taking measures to prevent plastic pollution is key for environmental conservation. While knowing that waste management is not practiced by many due to laziness/lack of time, making an effort to use less plastic products and disposing waste appropriately in public places are minimal and time effective steps for environmental conservation. Also, the inclusion of globalization and sustainability as an underlying concept made the foundation for understanding environmental innovation in the modern day with preserving the interests of other fields such as business and economics and their contribution in understanding the environment. Destructive impact on the environment bought both gradual and vigorous change for reform and improvement in the safety of humans and other organisms. As a conclusion, knowing about innovative practices and solutions that are time effective requiring less resources improved the level of sustainability; however, it is still a matter of taking action and implementation rather than consistent persuasion and nudging. It is a natural tendency for difficulty to take place when thinking long term (which causes lack of immediate action). Apart from being socially responsible, decision and planning skills for future outcomes is pivotal in other aspects of life as well from school to work apart from limiting plastic waste and conserving the environment.
